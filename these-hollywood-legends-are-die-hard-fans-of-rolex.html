Rolex is undoubtedly one of the best-known luxury brands in the world. It is no surprise that many fans of the long-standing Swiss company are in the Hollywood celebrity circle. The rich and beautiful life tends to appreciate these wristwatches' prestige and impeccable artistry. One could always find celebrities with a Rolex around their wrist. Here is a sneak peek at a list of the best-paid actors and their Swiss luxury timepieces. These Hollywood celebrities wear Rolex watches, and what models gained the most popularity among the film crowd. Some lists may have different names. It depends on the parameters selected to evaluate the same.

The rich and famous have always flaunted their luxury accessories. Among them are the Rolex wristwatches that are a symbol of extreme luxury. The celebrities are ready to spend a fortune to gain possession of a Rolex. The company took great effort to reach the position it is in today. Years of continuous effort led the company to the prestigious position it holds today.

Dwayne 'The Rock' Johnson

Johnson is known for his liking of the bold watches made by Panerai, but it was revealed in an interview that he had been a huge fan of Rolex In the past. He fulfilled his dream of owning a Rolex during his wrestling career. His first Rolex was destroyed in the ring. Other models of Rolex later followed this until he became a fan of Panerai. As on Watch Master, one of his Rolexes cost $14,590.

Mark Wahlberg

Wahlberg is a fan of Rolex watches. He has a passion for collecting them. From the relatively down-to-earth models like the Daytona with a white dial, the celebrity's collection is highlighted by many versions of the Daytona. Further, his collection includes models like the Submariner and Day-Date models made of platinum. His watches retail at $40,450, while the market price is between $57,000 and $63,000.

Will Smith

Will Smith has been seen wearing a variety of wristbands for decades. His collection holds a Rolex Sky-Dweller in Everose gold on a brown leather strap, a Rolex Submariner in stainless steel, and a Rolex Heritage Black Bay with a bezel and a 41 mm case diameter. One of these is valued at $26,000.

Sofía Vergara

According to Monochrome, Sofia Vergara owns an 18k yellow gold model Daytona with a less common brown dial and mother of pearl subdials. Additionally, she owns several Datejust models, some of which are set with diamonds. Her Daytona costs around $40,000.

Robert Downey Jr.

Becker Time reports that Robert Downey Jr. has some exceptional watches in his collection. Of them, three are Rolexes. His collection of Rolex watches includes the famous steel Rolex Submariner nicknamed "Hulk" for its green bezel and green dial combo, a steel Milgauss with the unique green sapphire crystal, and a custom all-black PVD GMT-Master II. His Submariner costs around $13,497.28.

Reese Witherspoon

Reese Witherspoon wears a Day-Date In yellow gold with a domed bezel and gemstone indices. One would think that the timepiece is a bit extravagant, but Witherspoon wears the glamorous wristwatch in the most endearing way possible. The watch has a price of about $24,000 to $43,000.

Jennifer Aniston

Time And Tide Watches say that Jennifer Aniston owns a blacked-out Rolex Milgauss. The stainless steel case has been treated with a DLC or PVC gloss black coating over its original satin and mirror polish finishes. Further, she has opted for a clear sapphire crystal over the factory green crystal. She also owns a yellow gold Rolex Day-Date and a yellow gold Datejust. These have a price range from $15,000 to $31,500.

Kevin Hart

Hart has several Rolexes in his possession ranging from diamond-set Day-Date models to Daytona watches in every variation. Steel with a white dial, a rare vintage model, a platinum timepiece with a blue dial, a brown bezel, and an extravagant Rainbow model all come in his collection. One of these is priced at $9,100.

Overall, watches have always been a way for Hollywood celebrities to flaunt their hard-earned money. In watches, Rolex is one of the most luxurious and preferred brands. Many Hollywood legends have been spotted wearing the iconic Rolex wristwatches. It is viewed as a symbol of social status and extreme luxury.

Rolex was not a luxury brand from the start. The Swiss watchmaker reached its current status with years of consistent efforts. Today, Rolex is one of the top symbols of luxury and class. The top celebrities wear it in every field. Rolex has reached a phase where the name is enough to recognize the brand. Celebrities purchase Rolex wristwatches to elevate their status in a crowd. Rolex watches have been featured in several films starring top actors. The brand has gained fans who dream of getting hold of a genuine Rolex wristwatch.

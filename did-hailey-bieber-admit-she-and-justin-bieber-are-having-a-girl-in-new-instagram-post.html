Did Hailey Bieber Admit She And Justin Bieber Are Having A Girl In New Instagram Post?

August 2024 · 4 minute read

Highlights

  • Fans speculate Hailey Bieber is expecting a girl based on her cherry blossom caption and the pink color of the flower.
  • The Biebers are ecstatic about their first child, seeking advice from celebrity mommy friends like Kylie Jenner.
  • Hailey's pregnancy journey has been highly anticipated, with fans eagerly awaiting any hints about the baby's gender and name.

Hailey Bieber and Justin Bieber surprised everyone when they announced their pregnancy earlier this month (news that no one was expecting because, at the time, the couple were facing relationship rumors). Now, all eyes are on Hailey and her growing bump. So, when she took to Instagram recently to share a collection of photos, fans took notice. Her pictures included images of her growing belly, close-ups of her face, pictures of her food, and even a sweet snap of her and the Stay singer.

But was this post more than a random collection of images? Fans seem to think so, and the clue it seems is in Hailey's caption. "Little cherry blossoms on my nails little cherry blossom in my belly," she wrote. OK, but what does this mean?

Fans React To Hailey Bieber's Cherry Blossom Caption

The cherry blossom is a pink flower, and pink is a color often used to announce that an expected mother is carrying a girl. Fans are now convinced the model will have a girl! The comment section of the post is filled with messages about whether this is a surprise gender reveal. "Is it a girl? Are you going to name her blossom? Or a flower name?" a fan commented.

Other comments on the post include, "A GIRL CONFIRMED AHHHHHH,” "baby bieber is a girl," and "OK, IT’S A GIRL!" Some fans are also convinced the reference to the cherry blossom could be a hint at the name. "Blossom Bieber," a fan wrote. "CHERRY BIEBERRRR???" another fan asked. It could be either of these names or neither. We will just have to wait to find out.

What Has Been Said About Hailey Bieber's Pregnancy?

Hailey Bieber and Justin Bieber are thrilled to be expecting their first child. As they prepare for this life-changing event, the model is reportedly asking her mom friends for advice. "She's been turning to her celebrity mommy friends for advice, like Kylie Jenner and the Kardashians," a source recently told Us Weekly. "This is a completely new chapter for Hailey and Justin."

The insider also referred to the Biebers as being "super excited" about their child. "This is what they wanted for so long," they said. "Their prayers have been answered."

The milestone comes almost six years after the couple married in 2018. They are also fortunate to have a great support system and are friends with many couples who already have children (not only does this mean future baby play dates, but also someone who they can talk to about parenting, if they so choose).

Although she always knew she wanted to have a baby, she didn't want to rush things after getting married." The Biebers kept their secret for as long as possible, and it was reportedly not easy because Hailey "couldn't wait to make the big announcement."

Could Hailey Bieber's new Instagram post also be a gender reveal? Her fans certainly think so, but it's all speculation at this point. However, it does give a glimpse into the future and how much attention there is going to be on baby Bieber.

Has Selena Gomez's Mom Mandy Teefey Liked Any Of The Star's Confirmed And Alleged Ex-Boyfriends?

July 2024 · 5 minute read

Selena Gomez is currently single, making tons of money from Instagram, and acting in interesting projects like the thriller film Spiral. Fans are waiting to see if she will fall in love again. However, people are always interested in her past relationship with Justin Bieber. And she has dated quite a long list of other stars.

Selena Gomez's bond with her mom Mandy Teefey has been written about a lot. They produced the Netflix series 13 Reasons Why together. Did Mandy Teefey love Justin Bieber? And how did she really feel about some other celebrities who her daughter dated?

What Did Selena Gomez's Mom Mandy Teefey Say About Justin Bieber?

It's never easy for a parent to see their child feeling any pain at all. And when Selena Gomez dated Justin Bieber in the 2010s, Mandy Teefey had a lot to say about this relationship.

Bieber and Gomez got back together in 2017. Whenever a couple reunites, it's only natural that their friends and family might be worried about how things are going to go the second time around.

Mandy Teefey wasn't a fan of Justin Bieber, according to Us Weekly. It sounds like she was very concerned about whether the relationship was good for her daughter.

In 2017, Us Weekly reported that Bieber didn't celebrate Thanksgiving with Gomez's family that year. A source said that Gomez felt Teefey "still has trust issues." The source said, “There is a lot of uncertainty about him as far as Selena’s family is concerned. He is really hoping that they give him another chance, but he knows that it is going to take some time."

In 2018, Mandy Teefey was "not happy" that Gomez and Bieber reconnected. According to People, she was interviewed by Gossip Cop and shared what she was really feeling.

Teefey said, "Selena can live her life however she wants as long as she is happy, safe and healthy. She is 25 years old and knows what is at stake with her health. I do not control her the way it has been portrayed. Selena is an adult and can make her own choices.”

In 2018, E! News reported that Gomez and Teefey decided that they shouldn't discuss Bieber. When the publication inquired about their relationship status, Teefey explained, "You would know before I did. We don't talk about it."

Several sources say that when the couple began seeing each other in 2017, it was a big problem for Gomez's mom. It's possible that this caused a lot of stress and made it tough for the pair to make it. It couldn't have been easy for Gomez to hear what her mom had to say.

But since the couple was so young when they dated, that could be why they broke up for good. It's hard to say that Mandy Teefey's feelings led to the final breakup.

What Did Selena Gomez's Mom Mandy Teefey Say About Her Other Ex-Boyfriends?

According to Cosmopolitan, Selena Gomez was in a relationship with Nick Jonas in 2008, Taylor Lautner in 2009, and Niall Horan and Zedd in 2015. In 2016, she went out with Charlie Puth and Samuel Krost, who worked for the Fyre Festival and was in charge of getting bands to agree to perform at the ill-fated event. The singer also went out with The Weekend in 2017.

People also think that Selena Gomez and Orlando Bloom were involved, but they haven't said anything about it.

Did Selena Gomez's mom Mandy Teefey comment on any of these relationships?

It seems that she definitely commented the most about Justin Bieber. Since Gomez and Bieber were together for such a long time, that's only logical. It does seem that Mandy Teefey had an opinion on Gomez dating The Weeknd.

According to Ace Showbiz, there was a report that Teefey didn't think that Gomez should be with The Weekend. A source explained in an interview with Hollywood Life, "Selena's family is so incredibly supportive and loving. She has a big extended family and they all support her. When she was struggling they showered her with love constantly. They helped her get back on her feet and solid ground."

However, when The Weeknd shared a photo of him and Gomez at the Met Gala, Teefey seemed pleased. Us Weekly reported that she wrote on the Instagram post, "Glowing, smiling, looking health [sic] and equal love. Mama is happy XO."

When people noticed that Teefey had shared her opinion, she said, "Yes, I am happy my daughter is happy," according to Elle.

It doesn't seem that Mandy Teefey commented on Selena Gomez's relationships with Taylor Lautner or Nick Jonas. From what she has said about The Weeknd and Justin Bieber, Selena Gomez's mom seems to care about her daughter's mental health. And it's only logical that if she sees that Gomez is having a tough time in a relationship, she wouldn't be on board. Any parent would want to help their child through a situation that was making them sad.

Even though Selena Gomez hasn't had a perfect bond with her mom Mandy Teefey over the years, her mom definitely cares about her. In 2022, Teefey was interviewed on the podcast On Purpose that Jay Shetty hosts. She said that she wasn't going to see Gomez's documentary My Mind And Me because it would be challenging and upsetting. In the movie, Gomez talks about having lupus and bipolar disorder.

Teefey said, "We went through that and found healing. We've moved past some of it, and even if it's something she went through, and I didn't know she went through it, as a mother, it's going to affect me. It's going to hurt my stomach and put me in that mindset. I'm going to wish I could go and protect her."

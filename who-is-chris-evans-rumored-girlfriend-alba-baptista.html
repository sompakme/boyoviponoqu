Who Is Chris Evans Rumored Girlfriend Alba Baptista?

August 2024 · 5 minute read

Chris Evans has been linked to several women in the last two years. Still, he has never confirmed any of the rumors. Sure, he once claimed to be the father of Lizzo's baby but that's as far as we can get. Back in 2021, fans were convinced the Marvel Cinematic Universe star was dating Selena Gomez who was eventually linked to Neymar Jr. after it was revealed that fans faked evidence of her rumored romance with Evans.

There is a weird fixation on getting the actor off the market that recently, fans "found evidence" (again) that he's dating the Warrior Nun star Alba Baptista. Here's everything you need to know about her and the alleged proof that she's dating the Captain America actor.

Who Is Alba Baptista?

Baptista is a Brazilian-Portuguese actress who currently resides in Portugal. The 24-year-old is said to be multilingual. She obviously speaks Portuguese but is also fluent in English, Spanish, French, and German. She's popularly known for her English-language debut, the Netflix series Warrior Nun which premiered in July 2020. But Baptista's career can be traced back to when she was 16. Her first acting gig was in 2012 in Simão Cayatte's short film Miami. Her performance got her the Best Actress Award at the Festival Ibérico de Ciné. That's quite a huge feat for a newcomer.

The actress has also starred in several Portuguese films like Caminhos Magnétykos and Equinócio, and Patrick, as well as other TV series like Jardins Proibidos, A Criaçao, and Jogo Duplo. Before she was known for her role as Ava Silva in Warrior Nun, she was most recognized for playing the main character Beatriz Varela in the Portuguese telenovela, A Impostora from 2016 to 2017. Based on various reports online, Baptista's current estimated net worth is $1,000,000.

With Warrior Nun getting a season 2 renewal, we're sure Baptista will soon reach greater heights in her career. She's also quite the dedicated action star, so a Hollywood action film may be right around the corner. "I arrived a couple weeks early to meet the crew and get settled in but primarily to start stunt training," she told Parade of her preparation for the series' intense fight scenes. "We rehearsed overall combat training, but my physical training was more focused on Wire work [special effects training]." In the same interview, she revealed that her current celebrity crushes are "Robert Pattinson and Kristen Stewart… ironically." Interesting.

Who Has Alba Baptista Dated In The Past?

If you look up Baptista's current relationship status online, you'll see blogs saying that she's still in a relationship with Justin Amorim whom she first met on the set of the Portuguese film Leviano in 2014. However, it seems like their relationship may have eventually ended in 2020 or 2021. The Warrior Nun star is not one to talk about her dating life, so it's really hard to tell. Then in May 2021, she was spotted on vacation with Emily in Paris lead actor Lucas Bravo. It instantly sparked dating rumors.

The speculations were put to rest when fans realized that they're both starring in the upcoming 2022 film, Mrs. Harris Goes to Paris. Without any more public sightings of the two together and Bravo previously stating he'd never date a co-star, fans presumed that the pair might as well be just friends. "Once you step on set, it's a workplace, and I want to keep it professional," the French actor said of on-set affairs in an interview with Elle Australia in December 2021. He's not opposed to dating fans, though. "I don't really have fans, so I don't know," he humbly answered when asked about the possibility of dating a fan.

Is Alba Baptista Really Dating Chris Evans?

So far, Baptista and Evans' rumored relationship remains a fan theory. However, it's hard to look away from the evidence. It all started with internet sleuths noticing that Evans had started following Baptista on Instagram in fall 2020. But it wasn't until June 2021 that she followed him back. Now, the actress is also following the actor's brother Scott Evans and his dog's account. Fans were quick to put the puzzle pieces together.

It is now believed that Evans may have met Baptista in Europe last summer while he was shooting his upcoming 2022 movie The Gray Man and she was filming season 2 of Warrior Nun. Fans grew more suspicious when they saw Baptista post New Year's Eve photos that looked like they were taken in Evans' backyard. The actress reportedly spent the holiday in Los Angeles.

The most recent "proof" of their romance is Evans' stay in Lisbon during the last week of January. They think he visited Baptista there because of a video he shared on his Instagram Stories. They said the background of the clip matches the rooms at the Four Seasons in Lisbon. The actor has reportedly switched hotels since the theory went viral. As of this writing, neither party has confirmed the rumors.

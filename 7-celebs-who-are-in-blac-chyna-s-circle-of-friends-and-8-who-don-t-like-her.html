7 Celebs Who Are In Blac Chynas Circle Of Friends (And 8 Who Dont Like Her)

August 2024 · 6 minute read

Things couldn’t have been better for Blac Chyna when she announced she was dating Rob Kardashian in January of 2016. However, following their split just one year later, it seemed as if the TV star had found herself making a lot of enemies in Hollywood - including the Kardashians.

It’s not been an easy journey for the mother of two, who was said to have been heartbroken after her ex-boyfriend, Tyga, dumped her to be with Kylie Jenner.

Many assumed that Chyna was trying to get back at Kylie by getting with her half-brother and subsequently having a baby with him. Whatever her intentions were, Blac Chyna has burned bridges in Hollywood that she’ll never be able to rebuild. But it goes without saying that despite the scandals, Blac Chyna hasn’t necessarily lost every friend.

The TV personality mostly sticks with the people who have been by her side for years, which explains her close bond with the likes of Amber Rose and Nicki Minaj.

But who else is part of her circle of friends...and who isn’t?

15 Kylie Jenner - Feud

Kylie Jenner has never really been friends with Blac Chyna, even when they appeared to squash their beef and pose for a selfie, just to make fans think everything was fine between the two.

The truth is that Kylie had an issue with Chyna going after her half-brother, Rob, which seemed like a tactical move to get back at the reality star for “stealing” her man, rapper Tyga.

14 Rob Kardashian - Feud

Meanwhile, Rob Kardashian has been feuding with Chyna ever since the former couple went their separate ways for good, just over two years ago. In January of 2020, however, the USC graduate took things to new heights by filing for primary custody of their daughter, Dream.

Rob claimed in court documents that Chyna has a problem with substance and alcohol abuse.

13 Tyga - Feud

Blac Chyna’s other baby daddy, Tyga, isn’t on good terms with her, either. Aside from their feud, which erupted during his romance with Kylie, Chyna had also blasted the “Rack City” hitmaker for allegedly failing to make child support payments on time.

Tyga and Chyna aren’t friends, but they’re trying to make things work for the sake of their son.

12 Alexis Skyy - Feud

It’s not clear what initially ignited the feud between Blac Chyna and reality star, Alexis Skyy, but following their scuffle in January 2019, it was apparent that the two were better off keeping their distance from one another.

Alexis went on to spark romance rumors with Rob Kardashian, just days later when sock designer Rob live-streamed her doing a little cooking in his kitchen.

11 Soulja Boy - Feud

Just weeks after going public with their romance, Blac Chyna and Soulja Boy called it quits. They split in February of 2019. Unbothered by the split, the “Crank Dat” star retweeted an article about the split, along with a caption which indicated that the only thing that had interested him about Chyna was a certain body part.

Though he went on to apologize for the comment, Blac has completely shunned him from her life.

10 Future - Feud

Future and Blac Chyna had been seeing one another for a couple of months, but when she went public with their romance by getting his name tattooed on her hand, the rapper was quick to call it quits. Then, he made it clear he was single on Instagram.

A humiliated Chyna later got the tattoo removed - what an embarrassing thing to go through.

9 Jonathan Cheban - Feud

Though Jonathan Cheban was initially said to have gotten along with Blac Chyna, once she sued the Kardashian family and allegedly got physical with Rob, Kim’s best friend was done with her.

He even branded Chyna and her pal Amber Rose “ghetto street bullies” for how they treated Kylie while she was with Tyga.

8 Nicki Minaj - Friends

Nicki Minaj and Blac Chyna have been friends for years. Chyna is a former exotic dancer who has appeared in several of Nicki’s music videos, including 2010’s "Monster" and 2012’s “Come On A Cone.”

While it’s unclear how they first met, Nicki and Chyna have always supported one another in public and behind the scenes, which is refreshing to see amongst women in Hollywood.

7 Amber Rose - Friends

Amber Rose has been one of Chyna’s best friends since Chyna moved to Los Angeles. It was Amber who helped the reality star settle into her new home in Cali, while slowly but surely building her cosmetics empire. They’re both moms now, and when they do get together, it’s awesome. Even their kids bond with one another.

6 Wendy Williams - Friends

Following her divorce from Kevin Hunter, Wendy Williams admitted that the single life has been treating her well. Through mutual friends, she was introduced to Blac Chyna, who has become one of her closest pals.

According to the talk show host, she’s even been to Chyna’s home in Woodland Hills, California, and met her two children, King Cairo and Dream.

5 The Game - Friends

It’s no secret that Blac Chyna and The Game (he's a rapper) once shared a fling. But it’s believed that, even though they’re no longer romantically involved with one another, Chyna and Game have remained close friends.

They’re often seen partying together at the same events, and while some may think running into a former flame is awkward, the mother of two doesn’t see it as a big deal - especially when she still gets along with a guy.

4 Joseline Hernandez - Friends

It’s not surprising that Joseline Hernandez is friends with Blac Chyna. The two were often seen partying at the same events and they both happen to follow each other on social media.

Fans have also noticed how Joseline often refers to Chyna as her “wife” under her Instagram photos, so their friendship must be pretty close.

3 Cardi B - Friends

Before Cardi B blew up with her smash hit, “Bodak Yellow,” it was Chyna who would often play the rapper’s music on her Instagram Story - many people say that's the reason why Cardi received so much attention to start with.

Of course, that’s an arguable statement, but Cardi did notice Chyna’s support and thanked her for it on social media. The two have since followed each other on Instagram.

2 Tanisha Thomas - Friends

While it’s unclear whether they are friends in real life, Tanisha Thomas certainly gives off the impression that she’s cool with Blac Chyna. The former Bad Girls Club star often praises Chyna on her Instagram page, and while that doesn’t necessarily mean they are the best of pals, it’s nice to see women in the industry supporting one another.

1 T.I. & Tiny - Friends

It seems as if T.I. and Blac Chyna have been friends for quite some time. During a heated social media rant in July 2017, Rob Kardashian alleged that his ex-girlfriend previously shared very personal and private moments with the rapper and his wife, Tiny Harris.

While Chyna never addressed her supposed fling with the couple, it was public knowledge that she'd been 'friendly' with the married pair for a while.

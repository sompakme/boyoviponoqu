Taylor Swift Praised For Tipping Big While Watching Travis Kelces Game

September 2024 · 3 minute read

Highlights

  • Taylor Swift surprises fans by generously tipping food runners at the Chiefs vs. Patriots game.
  • Fans praise Taylor for acknowledging and thanking everyone around her, unlike many other celebrities.
  • This is not the first time Taylor has shown her generosity, as she previously gave out $55 million in bonuses to staff members and became a billionaire. #TaylorSwift #Generosity

Taylor Swift isn’t afraid to share her wealth after reaching billionaire status earlier this year. The singer was recently praised for tipping with $100 bills while watching her boyfriend Travis Kelce play for the Chiefs.

While watching the Kansas City Chiefs vs. the New England Patriots game at Gillette Stadium on Sunday, December 17, a fan noticed Taylor handing out big bills while accepting food from employees as she watched the game from a suite alongside Travis’ entourage.

The fan quickly snapped a photo and uploaded it to X (formerly known as Twitter), where it has since gone viral.

“Taylor Swift handing out tips to the food runners after today’s Chiefs vs Patriots game!” the caption accompanying the image said.

Many people praised Taylor’s generosity in response to the candid photo.

“I’m not a die-hard swiftie but what I notice about her – she acknowledges and thanks everyone around her,” one person tweeted. “The security , restaurant staff as she leaves, any workers at stadium she passes she says hello.. etc. Not many at her celebrity level or even c level do that.”

“The fact that SHE HERSELF is passing them out and not one of her staff is crazy to me. She’s amazing,” another person added.

This isn’t the only time this year Taylor has been praised for being generous to those working for her. In August, it was revealed the singer had given out $55 million bonuses to staff members as she was about to close the U.S. leg of her Eras tour, which has broken records. So far, it’s estimated to be on track to make $5.7 billion, reportedly making it the highest grossing tour ever (and booting multiple countries’ economies).

The bonuses for truck drivers alone were $5 million, as each received $100,000 individually.

In October, it was reported Taylor was officially a billionaire following the re-recording of album 1989, released nearly a decade ago. Her net wroth is now estimated to be $1.1 billion. She’s making $13 million per Eras tour stop, not to mention profits from the success of the accompanying movie, meaning her net worth will likely continue to skyrocket in the new year. And given Taylor’s history of generosity, fans can expect she’s going to treat those around her well, financially and otherwise, should her reputation hold true.

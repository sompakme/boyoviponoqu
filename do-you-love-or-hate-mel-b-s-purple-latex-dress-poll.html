Do You Love or Hate Mel Bs Purple Latex Dress?: Poll

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Purple reign! Melanie Brown — aka Mel B — showcased every curve on her body in a skintight latex dress in Los Angeles on Wednesday, August 31. 

The America’s Got Talent judge, 41, headed to lunch at Serafina in a purple latex minidress with a nude inset, long sleeves and an open back that displayed an edgy tattoo above her left shoulder. To match her dress, she put on a sky-high pair of purple suede Christian Louboutin pumps. A few gold bracelets and round black sunglasses with mirrored silver shades served as her accessories. For most of the day, including her time in front of the AGT camera, the former Spice Girl wore her brunette hair in a structured topknot. But that evening, she let her locks cascade in a high ponytail that reached her shoulders.

This isn’t the only wild outfit that fans should expect from Mel B, the mom of Phoenix, 17, Angel, 9, and Madison, 5. Along with GEM, a trio that includes her fellow former Spice Girls Geri “Ginger Spice” Halliwell and Emma “Baby Spice” Bunton, she dropped a huge hint to her loyal devotees. The group — minus Victoria “Posh Spice” Beckham and Mel “Sporty Spice” Chisolm — thanked their fans in a July 8 YouTube video to celebrate the 20th anniversary of “Wannabe,” which dropped in 1996.

“We’ve been thinking, we have the best fans in the whole entire world. … We want to celebrate and have a party! And when we do, you’re all invited,” Brown said, slyly addressing the rumors of a reunion tour.

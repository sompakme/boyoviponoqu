Celebrities Who Bought Their Parents A House After Making It Big

August 2024 Â· 6 minute read

Growing up, most of us have parents who are more than willing to support our dreams and the same goes for celebrities. A lot of celebs tend to thank their parents for helping them get to this point in their careers, and because of that, these celebs are more than willing to give back to their parents for helping them follow their dreams.

RELATED: Celebs Who Lived With Their Parents Up Until Recently

Many stars didn't grow up with much, so one of the first things they'll buy for their parents once they've made it big is a house. What better way to give back to your parents who sacrificed so much for you than by buying them the house of their dreams? These celebs tend to agree.

10 Zayn Malik

While Zayn Malik is most known for being Gigi Hadid's baby daddy these days, he was once a member of the world's biggest boy band, One Direction. Growing up, Zayn, his parents, and all of his siblings didn't have much.

RELATED: Celebrities Who Became First Time Parents In 2021

His parents sacrificed a lot for him to follow his dreams when he auditioned for X-Factor, so much so that once One Direction made it big he bought them a house. You can see the emotional moment when he shows his parents their new house in the One Direction movie, This Is Us.

9 Chris Pratt

Chris Pratt had to work hard like many other celebrities to get to the extremely successful point in his career. After he booked his first big gig when he was just getting started, he was just 23 years old. He took the money that he earned and decided to give back to his mom by buying her a house. While he didn't have the amount of money that he has today, he still used what he had for his mom.

8 Travis Scott

Rapper Travis Scott has said many times that his family had it tough while he was growing up. With a lot of hard work and support from his family, he was able to follow his dreams of becoming a musican and made them come true.

RELATED: Ariel Winter And Other Stars Got Emancipated From Their Parents

After making it big, he decided that he wanted to give back to his family. In 2016 he posted that he bought his family a house as a Christmas present, wanting them to have the luxury of a beautiful home that they didn't have when he was growing up.

7 Justin Bieber

Justin Bieber grew his career from the ground up, getting started as a child by posting videos of himself singing on YouTube. Once he eventually hit it big, he decided to give back to his father by buying him a $850,000 house located in Ontario, Canada. The house was for his dad along with his half-brother and half-sister, Jaxson and Jazmyn. While he was raised by his mother, Justin still has a close relationship with his dad and wanted to make sure that he was taken care of too.

6 Taylor Swift

Taylor Swift is one of the most famous musicians in the world, but she didn't get that way over night. Taylor has worked hard since she was a teen to become a successful musican, and with the help of her parents she was able to get to the level of fame that she has today. To say thank you for all that they've done, Taylor bought her parents a beautiful mansion worth $2.5 million located in Nashville, Tennessee. The gorgeous house is just a small token of her appreciation for everything they've done for her.

5 Cardi B

Cardi B is one of the most famous female rappers out there today, and she worked hard and hustled to get there. For years, Cardi dreamed of buying her mom a house in New York (since Cardi grew up in the Bronx), and in 2020 she got to make that dream a reality. She saved up all of her hard earned money and finally was able to buy her mom the dream house that she had been wanting to get her for years.

4 Michael B. Jordan

Actor Michael B. Jordan wanted to pay his parents back for everything that they had done for him like many other celebrities dream of doing. He finally got to make that dream a reality when he bought a house for his parents back in 2016 in Sherman Oaks, California. In a twist, though, Michael actually lived in the house with his parents for four years until he finally decided to move out in late 2020. Buying them a house and then living with them was a way to not only give back but also to stay close to them.

3 Pete Davidson

Saturday Night Live star Pete Davidson is so close to his mom. Wanting to give back to her and pay her back for all the sacrifices she made for him, Pete bought her a four-bedroom home on Staten Island back in 2016. The house cost a pretty penny, as it came to $1.3 million. For a while, Pete was living in the furnished basement which he made his own apartment. He lived there for five years until recently when he moved out and moved into a nearby condo instead.

2 Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson

Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson first bought his parents a house back in 1999. Since then, though, his parents have divorced, but that hasn't stopped him from taking care of them since he is able to. For Christmas in 2018, he surprised his mom with the ultimate present: keys to a brand new house that he bought just for her. He posted a video of her getting her present on social media, and you could see the pure joy on both of their faces.

1 Rihanna

Rihanna has come so far since she was just a kid growing up on the island of Barbados. Since making it big not only in the music world, but in makeup and fashion too, she's given back to her mother by gifting her a gorgeous five-bedroom house located in Barbados. She brought her mom to tears when she gave her the house while appearing on Oprah's show. The moment was very emotional, and you could see how much Rihanna loves her mom.

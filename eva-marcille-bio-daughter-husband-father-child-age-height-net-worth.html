Eva Marcille belongs to the class of celebritieswho became prominent before the age of twenty. Marcille left college at the age of 19 to compete in the third cycle of America’s Next Top Model and later emerged as the winner of the competition. Her win, which came with prizes including a modeling deal with Ford Models, a CoverGirl cosmetics deal and a spread in Elle, marked the beginning of her career.

Beginning as an inexperienced model,Marcille has grown into a top-notch supermodel in the industry as well as an actress and television show host. In addition to walking the runway for various fashion brands, she has also appeared in over 20 television series. Her latest projects in the film industry are Fear Famous and The Real Housewives of Atlanta.

Age, early life and rise to fame

The elegant model is the daughter of MichelleYvette Smith (an African American) and Evan Anthony Pigford (a Puerto Rican). She was born to her mother, Michelle, on October 30, 1984 in Los Angeles, California.

Eva’s full name is Eva Marcille Pigford. However, she took the name “Eva Pigford” when she started acting. According to records, her middle name was coined from her grandmother’s name. It’s actually a combination of her grandma’s names, “Marjorie” and “Lucille”.

Discussing the model’s education, she had her elementary education at Raymond Avenue Elementary School and later at Marina Del Rey Middle School, from where she graduated from Washington Preparatory High School in 2002.

Marcille furthered her education at the prestigious Clark Atlanta University in Atlanta, Georgia, but retired from the institution of learning shortly after winning America’s hotly contested Next Top Model.

    Doctors and the FDA advise women to avoid using CBD or any other cannabinoid products during pregnancy. The main reason for this is that during pregnancy, CBD can affect female hormones — particularly estrogen — which is key in maintaining reproductive health.

    Although current research is far from conclusive, for the same reason, it’s recommended women avoid CBD gummies and other products while taking estrogen-based contraceptive pills.

    While moderate modulation of the endocannabinoid system can be beneficial to hormonal balance, taking CBD while pregnant, breastfeeding, or undergoing certain contraceptive treatments may disturb hormonal balance.

    Different Types of CBD

    CBD products can contain one of three variations of CBD, which are:

Dylan Penn Dating Vampire Diaries Star Steven R. McQueen

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Moving from vampire to vampire hunter! Dylan Penn — the gorgeous daughter of Sean Penn and Robin Wright — is officially dating Vampire Diaries star Steven R. McQueen just months after she was spotted canoodling with Twilight star Robert Pattinson. 

Penn, 22, and McQueen, 25, attended Sean Penn's charity gala together on Jan. 11, even posing for a few photographs. An eyewitness saw the couple leaving the Golden Globes weekend event when Penn complained that her feet hurt. 

"Oh really?" McQueen asked before scooping his lady love up and carrying her. Penn's boyfriend gave her a quick kiss on the lips before carrying her out of the party. 

Though the couple has kept it secret, a source close to them said this relationship has been going on for a while.

Shahs of Sunset Star Reza Farahan and Adam Neely Are Married

September 2024 · 1 minute read

They did it! Shahs of Sunset star Reza Farahan and Adam Neely are husband and husband! The pair married over the Oct. 3 weekend, a source confirms to Us Weekly.

The nuptials come after fans of the Bravo reality show watched Farahan call off the couple’s wedding at the end of last season as the cast visited Thailand.

Shahs costar Asa Soltan Rahmati shared a photo on Instagram on Saturday, Oct, 3, with the newlyweds and their dog, Marty, that showed Neely’s new wedding ring.

In another shot, she and Los Angeles realtor Farahan hit a drive-through, and his wedding ring can also be seen. “Rollin through the drive-through with my Boos,” she captioned the photo, adding the hashtag #welldone.

Neely and Farahan met at the gym and began dating in 2012. They got engaged in Palm Springs in August 2014 after Farahan popped the question over dinner.

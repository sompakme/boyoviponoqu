Wendy Williams 'In Love' With New CEO Boyfriend Mike Esterman

September 2024 · 3 minute read

Wendy Williams has seemingly confirmed she is smitten with her new beau, Mike Esterman. The talk show host shared a picture on Instagram of herself with her boss boo and friend Dr. Oz.

The mom of one captioned the snap: "I’m surrounded by love! How about you?"

Dr Oz - full name Mehmet Cengiz Öz - added in the comments: "Such a great night."

Earlier this month, Esterman entered "Date Wendy" on The Wendy Williams Show for a chance to date the legendary radio DJ. After getting down to the final three, the 56-year-old chose the CEO and real estate mogul to be her new suitor.

Williams then to social media to tell fans Mike had taken her out on the town. Sharing a snap on Instagram, Mike and Wendy were seen cuddling in the back of a cab.

“Mike & I are having fun!! I'm glad he’s a REAL gentleman,” Wendy wrote in her caption under the photo.

Mike runs his own company called Esterman.com, which is “a full-service celebrity booking agency specializing in celebrity event bookings, appearance placement and product endorsements,” according to his company’s website.

The company has worked with stars like Beyoncé and Alicia Keys. The Maryland, D.C businessman also flips houses for huge profits.

Wendy, meanwhile finalized her divorce from ex-husband Kevin Hunter in January 23, 2020, after 22-year marriage. It came to light in 2019, that Kevin fathered a child with his longtime mistress, Sharina Hudson.

"No," the mom-of-one replies.

"No?" a shocked Oz says in response.

"No? I don't trust it," Wendy says nonchalantly.

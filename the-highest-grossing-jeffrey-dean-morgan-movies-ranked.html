It's time to roll out the red carpet because we're about to dive into the world of Jeffrey Dean Morgan, Hollywood's resident heartthrob! This guy has been on the scene for a hot minute, and he's starred in some seriously epic flicks that have made bank at the box office. From being the ultimate zombie slayer to kicking butt in the DC Universe, Jeffrey's got some serious acting chops that have left audiences wanting more. So, if you're ready to find out which of his movies raked in the most cash, then buckle up, my friend, because we're about to go on a wild ride! Here are the top 10 highest-grossing movies in Jeffrey Dean Morgan's filmography. Don't forget the snacks, and get ready to swoon!

The Accidental Husband - 2009 ($22.7 Million)

Via Wikimedia Commons

In this romantic comedy, Jeffrey Dean Morgan plays Patrick Sullivan, a firefighter who finds himself at the center of a love triangle. Directed by Griffin Dunne and produced by Yari Film Group, the movie also stars Uma Thurman and Colin Firth. Despite mixed reviews, The Accidental Husband was a commercial success, grossing $22,707,064 worldwide. Morgan's performance as the charming and handsome firefighter was widely praised, and he brought a certain depth to the character that endeared him to audiences.

Solace - 2016 ($23.5 Million)

Via Flickr/Plex

This riveting movie directed by Afonso Poyart follows FBI agent Joe Merriwether (Jeffrey Dean Morgan) and his partner, Katherine Cowles (Abbie Cornish), as they seek the help of a retired physician with psychic abilities, John Clancy (Anthony Hopkins), to solve a series of bizarre murders. Morgan delivered a superb performance, showcasing his remarkable range as an actor. Solace is a psychological thriller that grossed an impressive $23,549,031 at the worldwide box office.

The Losers - 2010 ($29.9 Million)

Via Flickr/Blog de Superheroes

In this action-comedy movie, Jeffrey Dean Morgan played the role of Clay. Produced by Warner Bros. Pictures, it grossed $29.9 million worldwide. The plot revolves around a group of Special Forces operatives who are betrayed and left for dead by their superiors. They seek revenge and embark on a dangerous mission to clear their names. Directed by Sylvain White, The Losers features other stars like Zoe Saldana and Chris Evans.

The Unholy - 2021 ($30.7 Million)

Via Wikimedia Commons

In The Unholy, Jeffrey Dean Morgan delivers a spine-chilling performance as Gerry Fenn, a journalist with a dark past. The horror movie, directed by Evan Spiliotopoulos, takes viewers on a terrifying journey through a small New England town where a supernatural force has been unleashed. Despite mixed reviews from critics, The Unholy still managed to gross an impressive $30,792,024 at the worldwide box office.

Red Dawn - 2012 ($48.2 Million)

Via Flickr/Espinof

Jeffrey Dean Morgan played Tanner in this war movie. Produced by FilmDistrict, it grossed $48.2 million worldwide. Red Dawn follows a group of teenagers who band together to fight off a foreign invasion in their hometown. Morgan's character is a former Marine who helps train the teenagers to become guerrilla fighters. Directed by Dan Bradley, the film also features Chris Hemsworth and Josh Hutcherson.

The Possession - 2012 ($82.9 Million)

Via Flickr/WallpaperUP

In this horror movie, Jeffrey Dean Morgan played Clyde. Produced by Lionsgate, it grossed $82.9 million worldwide. The Possession tells the story of a young girl who becomes possessed by a malevolent spirit after purchasing a mysterious box at a yard sale. Morgan's character is the father of the girl and will stop at nothing to save her from the clutches of the demon. Directed by Ole Bornedal, the film features other stars like Kyra Sedgwick and Madison Davenport.

P.S. I Love You - 2007 ($155.8 Million)

Via Flicker/HubPages

As William in this romantic comedy-drama, Jeffrey Dean Morgan delivered an outstanding performance. Produced by Warner Bros. Pictures and directed by Richard LaGravenese, it grossed $155.8 million worldwide. P.S. I Love You tells the story of a widow who receives letters from her late husband, guiding her on a path to rediscovering herself. Morgan's character, William, is the protagonist's best friend, who supports her through her journey. With Hilary Swank and Gerard Butler as co-stars, this movie will leave you both laughing and in tears.

Watchmen - 2009 ($186.9 Million)

Via Flickr/Covers.Box

As Edward Blake/The Comedian, Jeffrey Dean Morgan delivered a standout performance in this superhero movie. Produced by Warner Bros. and directed by Zack Snyder, it grossed an impressive $186.9 million at the worldwide box office. Set in an alternate reality where superheroes exist, the watchmen are forced to reunite to save the world from an impending war. With Malin Akerman, Billy Crudup, and Jackie Earle Haley, Watchmen is an action-packed thriller that will keep you on the edge of your seat.

Rampage - 2018 ($427.9 Million)

Via Flickr/JoBlo

Jeffrey Dean Morgan played Agent Russell in this adventurous movie. Directed by Brad Peyton and produced by New Line Cinema, it grossed an incredible $427.9 million at the worldwide box office. The plot revolves around three monsters that destroy cities and Agent Russell's attempts to stop them. Morgan's character is an Agent of OGA who is tasked with neutralizing the monsters. With Dwayne Johnson and Naomie Harris co-starring, Rampage will leave you thrilled and on the edge of your seat.

Batman V. Superman: Dawn of Justice - 2016 ($872.3 Million)

Via Flickr/Undead Walking

In this epic superhero movie directed by Zack Snyder, Jeffrey Dean Morgan played Thomas Wayne, the father of Bruce Wayne, aka Batman. The film follows the clash between Batman and Superman as they face off against each other in a fight for justice. With a star-studded cast that includes Henry Cavill, Ben Affleck, and Gal Gadot, Batman v. Superman: Dawn of Justice grossed an impressive $872.3 million at the worldwide box office. Morgan's portrayal of Thomas Wayne was praised for adding emotional depth to the story.

Early Life

In the early days of his journey to become one of the most successful DJs in the world, DJ Zedd, born Anton Zaslavski, had a humble beginning. He was born on September 2, 1989, in Saratov, Russia, before moving to Kaiserslautern, Germany, with his family at the age of three.

Raised in a musical household, Zedd’s passion for music was fostered from a young age. At just four years old, he began taking piano lessons, and by the time he turned twelve, he was already learning to play the drums. These early experiences and exposure to diverse musical instruments laid a solid foundation for his future career in the music industry.

While exploring his musical talents, Zedd drew inspiration from various genres and artists, embracing the vast world of music and honing his skills as a producer and DJ. Career

Zedd’s music career began in 2002 when he joined the German deathcore band Dioramic. However, he soon became interested in electronic music after discovering the French electro group Justice. After leaving Dioramic, Zedd gained recognition by remixing tracks by artists such as Skrillex, Justin Bieber, and Lady Gaga. His remix of Lady Gaga’s “Born This Way” was even included in her album of the same name.

Zedd’s breakthrough moment came with the release of his debut single “Clarity” featuring Foxes in 2012. The song achieved notable commercial success, reaching the top 10 on several music charts worldwide. This marked a significant turning point in Zedd’s career, establishing him as a prominent figure in the electronic music scene.

Throughout his career, Zedd has collaborated with numerous renowned artists across different genres. Some notable collaborations include “Stay” with Alessia Cara, “The Middle” with Maren Morris, and “Break Free” with Ariana Grande. These collaborations have not only expanded Zedd’s musical repertoire but also contributed to his crossover appeal and increased his fan base.

“Collaborating with other artists has allowed me to experiment with different sounds and styles. It’s always exciting to bring different creative visions together and see what we can create.” – Zedd

Zedd’s ability to seamlessly blend genres and create infectious melodies has earned him critical acclaim and multiple awards. He has received accolades such as Grammy Awards, Billboard Music Awards, and MTV Video Music Awards for his contributions to the music industry.

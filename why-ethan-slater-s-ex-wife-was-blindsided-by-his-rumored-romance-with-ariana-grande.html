In July 2023, it was revealed that Ariana Grande had split from her husband Dalton Gomez after she was seen attending Wimbledon without her wedding ring. Slater had previously been married to his childhood sweetheart with whom he had a child last year. A source opened up about their split, “She's doing her best to get back on her feet. She's incredibly smart and driven...she is truly the best mother.”

Ariana Grande's Shock Split From Husband

The "7 Rings" singer reportedly separated from her husband in January 2023, any photos from their wedding have since been removed from her Instagram. The pair began dating in January 2020 and announced their engagement just 11 months later. The couple wed in an intimate ceremony at their home in Montecito, California in 2021.

There's been speculation about their marriage since Ariana was seen at Wimbledon last weekend without her wedding ring. She was reportedly last seen wearing her ring in April at Jeff Goldblum's London concert.

Who Is Ethan Slater?

Ethan Slater started his career as an off-Broadway actor. He made his Broadway debut as the titular character in SpongeBob SquarePants from 2017 to 2018. He won a Drama Desk Award for the role and was nominated for a Tony.

He went on to appear in off-Broadway with roles in Camelot, Assassins and Goodnight, Oscar before joining Grande in the big screen adaptation of Wicked. He is joined by Grande, Michelle Yeoh and Cynthia Erivo.

An insider told Us Weekly that the pair connected on set and weren't shy about their budding romance. “Ariana and Ethan weren’t careful,” the source revealed. “They’d hold hands on set in between takes. After news of Slater's relationship with Grande became public, the Broadway star set his Instagram account to private.

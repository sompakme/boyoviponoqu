Barbara Gauthier WTVM News Leader 9

Gauthier is a vastly recognized broadcast journalist. Currently, she serves as a news anchor at WTVM News Leader 9. Prior, she worked reported and anchored for more than 20 years at WKYC Channel 3 News in Cleveland.

Likewise, Barbara has also worked as a weekend anchor and reporter for WXIA-TV. It is known that she joined the station during the 1996 Olympic Games. Afterward, she joined BET in 2000 as the network launched its nightly newscast.

While here, Gauthier became the Southeast Bureau Chief for the network. Moreover, she provided reports for BET Nightly News and CBS News from the Atlanta area. She has also worked at television and radio stations in Hartford, Dallas, Texas, Stamford, and Connecticut.

Citing her work, Gauthier has had extensive experience with national and local news coverage on her resume. She started her journalism career, she began out as the Chief National Correspondent for the show.

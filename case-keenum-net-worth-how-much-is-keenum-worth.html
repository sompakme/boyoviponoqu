Collegiate Career

Case Keenum’s college football journey took place at the University of Houston, where he left an indelible mark on the field. During his collegiate career, Keenum set multiple NCAA passing records, solidifying his reputation as a formidable quarterback.

In 2008, Keenum became only the second player in the history of the University of Houston to surpass 5,000 passing yards in a single season. This exceptional feat showcased his incredible precision and skill on the field.

Under Keenum’s leadership, the Houston Cougars earned their first bowl game appearance in nearly three decades, a testament to his ability to elevate his team’s performance. Throughout his collegiate tenure, Keenum recorded a remarkable 48 total touchdowns and over 5,800 offensive yards.

Keenum’s outstanding college football career at the University of Houston set the stage for his future success in the professional league, laying the foundation for his impressive records and achievements.

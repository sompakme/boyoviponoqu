Kylie Jenner Shows Off Son Aire's Sneakers That Rival Her $1 Million Shoe Collection

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Highlights

  • Kylie Jenner spares no expense when it comes to her children's nursery and belongings, as seen in her son Aire's luxurious bedroom.
  • Aire's nursery features a $2,200 Louis Vuitton teddy bear and an extensive collection of shoes, rivaling his mother's million-dollar shoe collection.
  • Kylie's daughter Stormi has also been showered with extravagant gifts, including expensive toys, clothing, and birthday parties costing six figures.

Kylie Jenner has proven on more than one occasions she’s not afraid to spoil her children with the finer things in life. The reality star recently gave her fans a glimpse into her 1-year old son Aire’s nursery, and as is to be expected, it's a very luxurious child’s bedroom.

In a new YouTube video documenting Aire’s birth last year, Kylie showed off the baby’s nursery in her $36 million Los Angeles home. To start, the reality star showed off her son's oval-shaped crib in the center of the room, alongside a cozy chair adorned with a Louis Vuitton teddy bear worth $2,200. Kylie then went on to focus on Aire’s extensive collection of shoes, neatly arranged on numerous shelves in his closet.

It’s been reported that Kylie’s personal shoe collection is easily worth over $1 million, and features designer brands like Valentino, Christian Louboutin, and Dolce & Gabana. But given the extent of Aire’s shoe choices, it’s clear he's on his way to having a collection that rivals his mother’s in both price and selection.

Later in the video, Kylie’s eldest child, daughter Stormi, 5, entered the room and lovingly set down a homemade painting, presumably crafted for her younger brother, on the nightstand.

In other parts of the nursery, there were shelves brimming with children's books, stuffed animals, and various decorative items.

Kylie clearly spent a small fortune prepping her son’s nursery but given that Kylie’s net worth is close to $1 billion, her pricey choices aren’t surprising. The Hulu star has also made headlines various times over the years for buying her daughter Stormi equally expensive toys and clothing pieces.

Some of the most luxe things Kylie has splurged on for Stormi include a $12,500 Hermes back-pack her daughter’s first day of school, a Fendi stroller for $2,400, and a mini Hermes handbag for $24,000 – which Stormi was seen carrying when she was under 2 years old.

Kylie, who shares her two children with her ex-boyfriend Travis Scott, didn’t hesitate to drop six figures for Stormi’s first birthday. The makeup mogul reportedly spent $100,000 on the affair, which had a Stormiworld-theme – a nod to Travis’ Astroworld empire.

Moreover, Kylie apparently dropped another six figures for Stormi’s birthday the following year, which included renting out an entire studio space. Needless to say, the Kardashian-Jenners go all-out when it comes to their kids, especially where birthdays are concerned.

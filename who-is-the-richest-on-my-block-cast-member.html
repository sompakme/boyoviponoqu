Who Is The Richest On My Block Cast Member?

July 2024 · 5 minute read

The Netflix hit series, On My Block shot into the spotlight right after its first season premiered in 2018. The show follows a group of street-savvy teens trying to get through high school while their bond is repeatedly tested. The last thread seasons of the smash hit left fans on their toes with major cliffhangers, and this same effect is what is served in the new season.

The show is in its fourth season, with the group in their final year of school which means that things might just be wrapping up for this teen drama. Season four features fan-favorite actors Julio Macias and Jessica Marie Garcia. On My Block has been making waves over the years, and we cannot help but wonder just how much the fame has earned the cast members. Here’s a rundown of the richest On My Block stars.

8 Brett Gray - $400,000

Brett Gray made his way into the movie industry through a short film called Tortoise back in 2015. Gray is a successful actor who has featured in different projects including the hit series When They See Us. The star is also set to voice the lead character in the forthcoming series, Star Trek: Prodigy. In addition to his success as an actor, Gray is also a musician and has been able to amass an estimated $400,000 net worth from his artistic talents.

7 Jason Genao - $400,000

Born in New Jersey, Jason Genao has been in the Hollywood scene for a few years. He made his debut screen appearance portraying William Reeves in the hit series Law & Order Special Victims Unit in 2016. After that, Genao went on to appear in other movies and Tv shows including Logan, God Friended Me and The Get Down. Genao is doing well for himself and is estimated to be worth $400,000.

6 Diego Tinoco - $500,000

For Diego Tinoco, acting started out as a casual hobby which grew into the full-fledged career it currently is. Tinoco made his debut television appearance in 2016 playing the role of Mateo in the hit series Teen Wolf. Shortly after, he scored roles in a couple of movies and shows including Mourning War, Drizzle of Hope, R#J, and Welcome to Valhalla. Apart from being a successful actor, the star is also a professional model. Tinoco has also stated that he is interested in making music and might pursue the career path later in his life. Tinoco has earned himself an estimated net worth of $500,000.

5 Julio Macias - $500,000

Julio Macias started his acting career in 2016 when he scored a role on Nickelodeon’s Game Shakers. Shortly after Macias featured in a few other shows including Jane The Virgin and Por Sofia before getting his big break playing Oscar Diaz in Netflix's On My Block. In 2020, Macias landed the main role portraying Pete Astudillo in Selena: The series. The star currently has an estimated net worth of $500,000.

4 Jessica Marie Garcia - $1 million

Kicking off her acting career in both television and film in 2010, Jessica Marie Garcia has made a name for herself in Hollywood. She rose to fame with her role in the comedy series Liv and Maddie as Winifred “Willow” Cruz. Shortly after, the star went on to land roles in television series including Hacking High School, How to Get Away With Murder, American Princess, and Diary of a Future President. Garcia is set to make her debut appearance on the fourth season of On My Block playing Jasmine. Presently, she is estimated to be worth $1 million and also happens to be one of the richest cast members on the show.

3 Sierra Capri - $1 Million

Shortly after making her debut TV appearance in On My Block, American actress Sierra Capri shot into the limelight. Capri landed roles in a couple of movies including Neighbors 2: Sorority rising, American Skin, and Hidden Figures. Her talents behind the camera have earned her an estimated net worth of $1 million.

2 Peggy Blow - $1.5 million

Peggy Blow is an American actress with German origins who has been in the film industry for over three decades. Even though she had been included in several projects before, she came into the limelight with her role as Ruby’s grandmother in Netflix’s On My Block. Over the years she has featured in many other series including The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air, The Jeffersons, Hangin’ With Mr. Cooper, and Seinfeld. Blow is estimated to have a $1.5 million net worth.

1 Emilio Rivera - $5 million

Veteran American actor Emilio Rivera made his first television appearance in 1992, where he starred in the show Renegade. Ever since, Rivera has gone on to star in several other shows including The Bold and The Beautiful , 90210, Texas Ranger, Beverly Hills, NYPD Blue, My Name Is Earl. In 2008, Rivera scored a recurring role portraying Marcus Alvarezin the hit series Son of Anarchy. Rivera’s success was not limited to television alone, he also featured in several movies over the course of his career including Spider-Man 3, The Cable Guy, he Dead one, and Strange Wilderness. He currently has an estimated net worth of $5 million which he made from his acting career.

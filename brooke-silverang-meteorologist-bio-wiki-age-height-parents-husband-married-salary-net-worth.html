Brooke Silverang Meteorologist

Silverang WINK News

She is a meteorologist at WINK-TV, she also works with the WINK News team from Monday to Wednesday from 9-10 am. Brooke joined Fort Myers Ares weather team in July 2017. Previous, before WINK News, she working in Lafayette, Louisiana for two years.

During her days at KLFY-TV, she served as a weekend meteorologist for Early Morning Passe-Partout Shows and Evening 6 pm and 10 pm shows across Acadiana, Southern Louisiana.

While still in Lousiana, she Covered the 2015 historic August flood that brought 30” of rain to South Central Louisiana, as well as Hurricane Irma in Southwest Florida.

Where is Brooke Silverang Now?

Silverang is the newest Meteorologist to join the WPBF 25 First Warning Weather team. She brings weather forecasts on weekend evenings. Furthermore, she has worked as a Meteorologist and Reporter at WMDT in Salisbury, MD, and in the heart of Cajun Country, Lafayette, LA at KLFY. Silverang was happy to return to her home state as a Meteorologist for WINK News in Fort Myers, FL where she covered Hurricane Irma and other storms over the past three years.

Dutch 2 Movie Release Date and Bet Plus Cast

July 2024 · 6 minute read

Dutch 2 movie release date is September 2023. The sequel to the 2021 thriller movie will be available on BET Plus.

The second movie will continue after the end of the first movie. The first movie ended with James escaping from the courthouse. As a result, his team was sent to jail.

However, the government law gives them another chance to live life again. Meanwhile, Angel takes back streets.

It is a Manny Halley Production and Faith Media Distribution. It follows the book story written by the author Teri Woods and Kwame Teague.

It is directed by Salvatore Sclafani, which is his USA directorial debut. He thanked all the amazing talent involved and the IMG team that made it all possible. The actor Mig Macario collaborated with the director.

Dutch 2 Movie Is Finally Coming Out

Dutch 2 Movie is to be released on Bet Plus in September 2023. The cast released a teaser trailer on their social media accounts.

The first movie in the series aired on March 12, 2021.

The highly anticipated movie trailer was shared by Markice Moore and DJ Akademiks on January 7, 2022, on social media.

They mentioned that the movie was ‘coming soon in 2023,’ which excited fans and generated a lot of buzz.

Recently, the actors involved in the movie have been posting numerous updates on their social media handles related to the film this July, indicating that the release date is approaching.

Although they haven’t clearly stated when the movie is coming out, these updates suggest that it probably be in this September.

Even this July, Lance Gross conveyed the trailer and captioned Is DUTCH alive? Dutch 2: Comming 2023.

What To Expect From Dutch 2?

It is based on the book Dutch II Angel’s Revenge by Teri Woods and Kwame Teague.

The Woods and Teague book story says Angel and One Eyed Roc, two of Dutch’s lieutenants, were captured and sent to prison for life.

A legal technicality overturned their sentences, and they got released with plans to recapture what they had lost.

It also shared some sides of the story in the movie, as seen in the trailer.

Angel and other criminals get out of prison. The inmate felt he is getting another chance to right his wrongs.

When the officer announced James’ demise, Angel takes the street back as she likes the challenge.

While at the end of the first part, Dutch escaped from the court and his sentence as he received help from his team. In this, many lost their lives while he flew in the helicopter.

So the series will continue with this ending and the return with the entry of the Dutch with his new mission.

In the 2005 book, they all lost their lives in the plane crash, except Angel, per readers.

Fans say they are into the movie because the first one was impressive. It was directed by Preston A. Whitmore II.

Dutch 2 Movie Cast

Dutch 2 movie cast includes Lance Gross and Rocsi Diaz. D’Kia Anderson and Markice Moore are the supporting cast members on the BET Plus movie.

Lance Gross As Benard James

Lance Gross plays the role of Bendard James, aka Dutch. His character is a dangerous criminal and the ruler of the streets.

The Oakland-born actor is known for his work in House of Payne, Sleepy Hollow, and Temptation: Confessions of a Marriage Counselor.

He first made an appearance in the Rihanna music video in 2005. Finally, he debuted in the TV series Eve as Choir Member.

Recently, the actor acted in House of Payne, Uthinkably Good Things, and Erax.

Rocsi Diaz As Angel Charlie

Rocsi Diaz portrayed the role of Angel Charlie. In the first movie, Sabella Ferreira performed the role.

So, fans are upset they replaced the original Angel from the Dutch 1 movie with Diaz.

However, the Tegucigalpa native felt grateful to be part of the movie. She added Angel made her dig into the past and relive moments she has always wanted to forget.

The character challenged Rocsi Diaz, and she thought it was only the beginning for her.

The actress is acknowledged for Cannonball, 106 & Park, and Entertainment Tonight.

She debuted in the TV series Brothers as Maxi in one episode in 2009. Besides this, she is a journalist and an Emmy Award Winning TV Host.

D’Kia Anderson

D’Kia Anderson starred as one of the leads in the movie.

She did not appear in the first film, but she received a great chance to be a member of the second one.

In 2009, she started her career with a small role in Kontrast as a Vip club girl.

She recently played in Cuddle Season, A House Divided, and Vullied No More. Her upcoming shows are The Wedding Invite, Peripheral, Deadly Desire, Pinnacle Heights, and Dark Matters.

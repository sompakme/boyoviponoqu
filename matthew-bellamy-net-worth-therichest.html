Mathew Bellamy is an English musician with an estimated net worth of $12 million. He has earned his net worth from being a lead vocalist, pianist, lead guitarist and also for being the main songwriter in the rock band called Muse. He is also one of the characters in the video game Guitar Hero 5.

Bellamy was born in Cambridge on 9th June 1978. He was the son of the top musician in pop musical group the Tornadoes. When he was 6 years old, he was actively participating in playing piano and by the time he was 11, he could play the guitar. His first musical performance was in 1991 where he played piano at Teignmouth Group Higher education.

He has had a successful career with Muse which also consists of other singers such as Dominic Howard and Christopher Wolstenholme. The band has successfully unveiled 6 albums which include Showbiz in 1999, Origin of Symmetry in 2001, Absolution in 2003, Black Holes and Revelations in 2006, Resistance in 2009 and 2nd Regulation in 2012.

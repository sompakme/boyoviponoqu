Ever since Sheryl Crow rose to fame during the early-'90s, she has consistently proven that she is a force to be reckoned with. Blessed with a beautiful voice and incredible songwriting skills, Crow is one of the most successful singers of her generations.On top of releasing a slew of hit songs over the years, it is worth noting that Crow also is still in great shape to this day. Even though Crow clearly deserves a partner of her caliber, it is still impressive to see the list of men she has been linked to.

8 How Much Money Is Josh Charles Worth?

According to whosdatedwho.com, Sheryl Crow and Josh Charles were involved for roughly three months in 2003 before going their separate ways.

Even though Charles has never risen to household name status, he has worked consistently for many years. Best known for his roles in shows like Sports Night and The Good Wife, Charles also starred in Dead Poet's Society alongside an extremely successful cast.

According to celebritynetworth.com, Charles has an estimated net worth of $8 million as of this writing.

7 How Much Money Is Lance Armstrong Worth?

When most people think about the people Sheryl Crow has dated, it is Lance Armstrong who comes to mind first and foremost. Together from 2003 until 2006, Crow and Armstrong were engaged to be married at the time of their eventual split.

At the height of his career, Armstrong was one of the most famous athletes in the world. A professional cyclist, Armstrong won the Tour de France seven times in a row. However, Armstrong's titles were later stripped away after he admitted to cheating for years.

Once Armstrong's cheating ways were revealed, his lucrative sponsorship deals went away. Despite that, celebritynetworth.com reports that Armstrong is still worth $50 million as of this writing.

6 How Much Money Is Owen Wilson Worth?

Considering that Owen Wilson easily is one of the most popular comedy stars of his generation, it makes sense that he has been linked to a lot of celebrities over the years. From 1999 until 2001, Wilson and Sheryl Crow were a couple.

An extremely successful movie star, Wilson has headlined movies like the Night at the Museum series, Wedding Crashers, Zoolander, and more. On top of those credits, Wilson also had a major role in many of Wes Anderson's films including Bottle Rocket and The Royal Tenenbaums.

Thanks to Wilson's movie star status, he has been able to demand a lot of money for his acting roles. According to celebritynetworth.com, that has allowed Wilson to accumulate a $70 million fortune as of this writing.

5 How Much Money Is Hank Azaria Worth?

Briefly linked in 2006, next to nothing is known about Sheryl Crow and Hank Azaria's reported relationship.

One of the stars of The Simpsons for almost three and a half decades as of this point, Azaria's main gig has been very lucrative. On top of that, Azaria has lent his talents to a long list of other projects including starring in the underrated show Brockmire.

According to celebritynetworth.com, Azaria has a $90 million fortune as of this writing.

4 How Much Money Is Kid Rock Worth?

After Sheryl Crow and Kid Rock collaborated on the song "Picture" together, there were a lot of rumors they were a couple which is why he has a place on this list. However, it has to be noted that both of them have repeatedly denied they ever were a couple.

A major recording artist who released several hit songs, Kid Rock also made a lot of money from extensively touring for many, many years. Thanks to both of those revenue streams, celebritynetworth.com estimates that Kid Rock is worth $150 million.

3 How Much Money Is Eric Clapton Worth?

Relatively early on in Sheryl Crow's incredible career, she was linked to Eric Clapton. Reportedly a couple from 1998 until the year 2000, Crow and Clapton remained friendly enough after their split that they later performed together.

An absolute music legend, Clapton has released an extremely long list of hit songs over the years including "Layla", "Tears In Heaven", "My Father's Eyes", and more. Most recently, Clapton released a song that rebelled against the COVID lockdowns.

Thanks to all of his hit songs and his years of touring, celebritynetworth.com reports that Clapton is worth roughly $450 million as of this writing.

2 How Much Money Is Ryan Seacrest Worth?

Another star who dated Sheryl Crow in 2006 according to the press, just like Hank Azaria, Ryan Seacrest wasn't linked to the talented singer for long.

Proof positive that hard work pays off, Seacrest seems to be one of the busiest people in the entertainment industry. An accomplished host, Seacrest is best known for American Idol, his radio show, and Live with Kelly and Ryan.

Related: Ryan Seacrest Is Worth $450 Million: Here Are 10 Ways He Makes His Money

Just like Eric Clapton, Seacrest has an estimated $450 million fortune as of this writing according to celebritynetworth.com.

1 How Much Money Is Steve Bing Worth?

Of all the people on this list, Steve Bing likely is the one that most people know the least. According to Wikipedia, Steve was an American businessman, philanthropist, film producer, and screenwriter before he passed away in 2020.

The grandson of Leo S. Bing, Steve inherited $600 million from his real estate tycoon grandfather. As a result, celebritynetworth.com estimates that Steve's estate is worth $600 million as of this writing.

On the other hand, in 2020 Town & Country reported that Steve's fortune had dwindled to just $300,000 in liquid assets by the time he passed away.
